Controls and drill explanation

Offense

Use the left stick to place the aiming reticle away from the defender. Press the displayed receiver button, then press Circle to click on. Steer with the left stick and use the displayed catch button as the ball arrives.

Defense

Press Circle during the pass to switch to the defender. Steer into the passing lane with the left stick and press Triangle as the ball reaches you.

Keyboard test controls

WASD = left stick, 1 = X, 2 = Circle, 3 = Square, 4 = Triangle, 5 = L1, 6 = R1.
