Why Join WURD?

What We Look For

  • Reliable users who communicate

  • No quitting, drama, or disrespect toward commissioners

How It Works

1. Join Discord

Enter the WURD recruit area with limited Recruit access.

2. Read Rules

Review the league rules and expectations before applying.

3. Complete App

The WURD bot will send you the recruit application link.

4. Get Reviewed

Commissioners review your application before moving you into Queue.

League Rules Snapshot

  • Communicate with your opponent
  • Respect commissioners and final rulings
